Orange Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • Zest of 2 oranges
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet baking powder
  • 1 packet vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat eggs and sugar until creamy.
  3. Add vegetable oil and milk, and mix well.
  4. Add orange zest and orange juice, and mix.
  5. Add flour, baking powder, and vanilla extract, and mix until smooth.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 35-40 minutes.
  8. Allow the cake to cool before slicing and serving.
